## 1. Luận điểm Đầu tư (Investment Thesis)

Vietcombank (VCB) is positioned moderately within the Vietnamese banking landscape. It demonstrates stable income growth and sound asset management practices. However, growth potential is tempered by rising cost structures and competitive pressure.

**Ba luận điểm chính:**

1. **Stability in Revenue Growth**: Recent growth rates in total operating income and NII indicate stability. VCB saw a mild increase in NII from 53,246 tỷ đồng in 2022 to 55,406 tỷ đồng in 2024.
2. **Efficiency Under Pressure**: The cost-to-income ratio has risen to 37.4% in FY2024 from 32.9% in FY2022, indicating increasing operational challenges that may affect profitability.
3. **Market Valuation Fair**: The current valuation metrics (P/B 2.26x) are aligned with peers, offering market-consistent returns but limited upside in the near-term.
